04 · Criteria

The goalposts cannot move

pre-committed · five tests
Lock Win Rate ≥ 65% High-conviction plays must clear this bar.
Live Win Rate ≥ 55% Qualified plays. Lower bar, smaller positions.
Average R ≥ 1.8 Net of realistic slippage.
Max Drawdown ≤ 25% Across the five-year simulation.
Positive Years 4 / 5 Including 2022 bear regime.
05 · Discipline

Why the rules exist

eight non-negotiables
Markets are noisier than props. Win-rate targets are lower. R/R targets are higher. The system enforces discipline. Discipline does not guarantee profit.
01
Score 71+ required. No exceptions for gut calls.
02
9:30 AM ET freeze gate. Board locks. No same-day changes.
03
−8% stop or break of setup level. Whichever first.
04
40% cash floor. Never deployed past this line.
05
Earnings within 5 days: automatic −30 score downgrade.
06
VIX above 25: automatic −15. High-vol regimes earn fewer plays.
07
$1K trading cap until backtest validates AND two weeks of live execution.
08
Every play logged. The ledger is the truth, not memory.
